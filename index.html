Quality You Can Count On

Cambria surfaces are backed by a Lifetime Limited Warranty to ensure you enjoy years and years of flawless service. To care for and protect your Cambria, a few precautions are recommended.

View Care & Maintenance Tips

  • Cleaning is Easy

    Cambria is nonporous and only requires warm water and a soft cloth to keep it looking like new. Using common household cleaners may damage the finish and mar the beauty of Cambria. See our full list of approved cleaning products. 

  • Turn Down the Heat

    Sudden temperature changes caused by hot pots and pans can harm any natural surface. Therefore, the use of a closed-weave hot pad or trivet is recommended. Reference our FAQs to help keep your Cambria in beautiful condition. 

  • Avoid Harsh Chemicals

    Cambria is made to stand up to the wear and tear of daily use for years of flawless performance. But even the hardest surfaces can be harmed by abrasive and corrosive materials. Find out more about product care and maintainance.
