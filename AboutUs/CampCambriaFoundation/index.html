Discover the Power of Camp Cambria

A life-changing experience for kids and teens with juvenile arthritis

Renovating for Today. Building for the Future.

To help create an even better experience for Camp Cambria™ kids, along with other children and adults with disabilities, the Camp Cambria Foundation, together with our generous partners, remodeled three key buildings at Camp Courage (home of Camp Cambria) from the ground up. The Camp Cambria Foundation is dedicated to raising awareness and funds to benefit the 324,000 kids and teens who are challenged by juvenile arthritis in North America.

Our Inspiration

 

Camp Cambria

Camp Cambria helps change lives—not just for a week, but forever—by giving kids
the opportunity to:

  • Meet other kids with juvenile arthritis, share stories, and build enduring friendships
  • Gain new tools for managing pain, stress, and other challenges
  • Learn new skills, gain confidence, and find joy
  • Enjoy canoeing, fishing, swimming, archery, horseback riding, and other activities
  • Sing, dance, and do skits around the campfire
  • Have the time of their lives!

Benefiting Children with Juvenile Arthritis


On August 13th, we were honored to host the 4th Annual Camp Cambria Classic™, one of Minnesota's most exciting charity events with a competitive and challenging golf tournament, as well as a VIP concert right on the shores of Lake Minnetonka.

100% of the proceeds from the Camp Cambria Classic benefit kids with juvenile arthritis through Camp Cambria and other initiatives. All expenses are donated and funded separately.
